The biggest compitition in club soccer is the champions league

This is the order in which the tournement takes place

  1. Group Stage
  2. Round of 16
  3. Quarter Finals
  4. Semi-Finals
  5. The Final

The UEFA Champions League takes place along side domestic leagues with entry requireing a top placement in a top european league or winning the tournement the year before.
